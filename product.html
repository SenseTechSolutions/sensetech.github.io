Classroom Simulator

The future of vision assistance

Immerse yourself in a VR experience of a classroom. Learn and get accustomed to navigating within classroom space with an actual walking aid. Experience the sounds inside a typical classroom, avoid hazards and feel how it is to attend a typical classroom day.

Normal vision? Experience the less fortunate by switching on our simulated eye conditions and try to live their challenges.
